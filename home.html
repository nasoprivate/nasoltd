About NASO

NASO Pvt Ltd is a leading marketer and distributor of branded frozen, fresh, chilled & dry Agro products in Middle East, Africa, Europe, CIS, Russia, etc having a direct reach & coverage of all market segments.

NASO Pvt Ltd Group is an international Trading & Manufacturing company specialized in the production and trading of high quality products in the Agro industry. We are currently serving more than 52 countries with our quality product & service in the field of Agro Import & Export

Our prime focus is to deliver the quality product as per as the International quality standard in all aspects by adhering the norms of ISO, HACCP, BSI, APEDA, Beureu Veritas,etc.

We understand that with quality , we also need the best pricing & delivery time. So our dedicated team is fully focused to deliver more then the customer expectation.
